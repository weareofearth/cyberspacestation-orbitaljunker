FACT SHEET

Description

Asteroid Hunter is a sci-fi story that takes place in a distant place, in the not-too-distant future.

Players assume the role of newbie trainees reporting to Cyberspace Command to log the required amount of hours in training sims in preparation for their rotation aboard the Orbital Colony Space Station L5 as active-duty crew members, tasked with keeping the galaxies clean and safe.

You are training, not gaming.

Blending a job sim with arcade gameplay into a casual RPG this experience tests your curiosity, hand-eye coordination, and whether or not you’ve got what it takes to qualify for the space program. 

Good luck, Space Cowboy!

Features

 Simulation:

   Log in to your Console, accept jobs, complete jobs, and get rewarded for your progress and accomplishments!

 Experience:

   Practice piloting a drone through a high-speed space highway while operating the asteroid-busting weapons system!

 Learn:

   Take a break from simulation training and read first-hand accounts from other pilot and operator crews, gather knowledge from mission reports, memorize tips and tricks, and stay up to date on the latest news from around the galaxy!
